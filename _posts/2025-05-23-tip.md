## What’s One Thing You’d Tell Someone Starting This Course?

I would say my biggest peace of advice is to think of writing code like writing an essay. Most of the process is similar; you have to plan out, make rough drafts, and revise your code. Approaching writing a piece of code like an essay helps a lot.

## What Did You Learn That Might Stick With You?

I think it helped me learn a lot about the basics. I got a much better understanding of data types and how they work. I also was able to understand things like for and while loops. 

## What Helped You Get Through Challenges?

I approached a lot of the coding games like they were a puzzle and it made it more fun rather than just viewing it as a homework assignment.

## What’s Next for You?

I am planning on learning data analysis skills so I will need to learn more CS for that.
